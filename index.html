What is FluxWallet.me?

FluxWallet.me is a multi-chain project platform, building Dapps and Games in an open-source fashion. This website serves as both a platform and a demonstration library for the projects FluxWallet.me is working on.


Join our Discord

FluxWallet.me has a Discord server, where participants, developers and users alike are free to join and discuss FluxWallet, their Dapps, games and open-source creations for chains such as ETC, ETH and TRX.
